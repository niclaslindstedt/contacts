type: Added
title: Fold the side menu footer away on wide screens for more list room
---

On wide screens where the sidebar is docked, a thin chevron rail now sits just
above the footer. Click it to **fold the footer away** — the Donate, trophy,
About, update, and Settings rows collapse, handing their vertical space to the
contact list so more contacts fit on screen. Click it again to bring the footer
back.

The choice is remembered across reloads. It only applies while the sidebar is
docked; the phone drawer always shows the footer.
