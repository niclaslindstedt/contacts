type: Added
title: Fold the side menu footer away for more list room
---

A thin chevron rail now sits just above the side menu footer. Click it to
**fold the footer away** — the Donate, trophy, About, update, and Settings rows
collapse, handing their vertical space to the contact list so more contacts fit
on screen. Click it again to bring the footer back.

The rail is available everywhere — both the docked sidebar on wide screens and
the phone drawer — and your choice is remembered across reloads.
