---
type: Changed
title: The emergency flag now lives only in edit mode
---

Marking a contact as an emergency (ICE) contact is done from the switch at the
bottom of the card's edit view — the **Mark as ICE** entry has been removed from
the side-menu right-click menu, since it's a set-once choice rather than a
per-row action. Flagged contacts still pin to the **In case of emergency**
section at the top of the menu, and still wear the badge.
