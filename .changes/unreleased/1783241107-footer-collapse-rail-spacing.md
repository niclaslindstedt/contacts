type: Fixed
title: Bigger footer-collapse control and no dead space when the footer is folded
---

The thin chevron rail that folds the side-menu footer away is now a
comfortably taller tap target instead of a hairline strip, so it's easier to
hit — especially on touch.

Folding the footer no longer leaves an empty gap below the rail: the collapsed
menu now reclaims that bottom inset and hands it to the scrolling contact list,
so folding the footer actually buys you more room for contacts.
