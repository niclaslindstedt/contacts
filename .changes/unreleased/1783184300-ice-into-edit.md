---
type: Changed
title: The emergency-contact switch moved into edit mode
---

The **in case of emergency** toggle no longer sits in the card header. It now
lives at the bottom of a card's **edit** view as a labelled switch, so it's set
once and stays out of the way rather than being always on show. Marking a
contact still pins them to the **In case of emergency** section at the top of
the side menu, exactly as before.
