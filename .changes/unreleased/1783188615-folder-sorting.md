type: Added
title: Sort folders alphabetically or by hand
---

Folders now have a **sort order** you control, under **Settings → General →
Folders**. Pick **Alphabetically** to keep your folders in name order
automatically, or **Manually** to arrange them yourself — drag one folder onto
another in the sidebar to drop it into place, with a thin line showing exactly
where it will land. Either way, folders still sit above your ungrouped contacts.

The choice applies both to the sidebar and the overview **List**, and the
hand-dragged order is saved on your address book so it syncs and travels with
your data like any other edit.
