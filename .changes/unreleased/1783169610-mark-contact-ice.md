type: Added
title: Mark a contact as in case of emergency and pin it to the top
---

A contact can now be flagged **in case of emergency** (ICE). Flagged contacts
are pinned to a dedicated **In case of emergency** section at the very top of
the side menu — no matter which folder they're filed in — so a next-of-kin or
first responder is always the first thing in reach.

Toggle the flag from the **siren** button in a card's header, or from a contact
row's right-click / long-press menu (**Mark as emergency contact** / **Remove
emergency contact**). A flagged row wears a small red siren badge wherever it
appears, so it's easy to spot.

The flag stays in the app — it isn't written to a vCard or CSV, which have no
field for it — but it's kept in the **JSON backup**, so restoring a backup
brings your emergency contacts back pinned.
