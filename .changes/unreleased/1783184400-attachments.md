type: Added
title: Attach files to a contact
---

You can now clip **files** to a contact — a restaurant's menu, a signed
contract, a scanned business card. Open a card's **edit** view, choose
**Add file**, and pick one or more files; each can carry an optional
**description**.

On the card, image attachments show as **thumbnails** you can tap to expand into
the same full-screen viewer the profile photos use (swipe between several).
Other files — PDFs and the like — list as rows: a PDF opens in a new tab, and
anything else downloads.

Attachments stay in the app (they aren't written to a vCard or CSV) but are kept
in the **JSON backup**. On a connected cloud drive they're filed out as real,
previewable files under an `attachments/` folder — the same way contact photos
are — so the synced document stays lean.
