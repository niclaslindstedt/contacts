type: Fixed
title: Hover highlight and pointer cursor on list, favorites, and archive rows
---

Contact rows on the **List**, **Favorites**, and **Archive** pages now
highlight on hover and show the pointer cursor over their clickable areas —
matching the side menu's rows, which already did. The sidebar folder rows also
had their name text sized down to `text-sm` so folders no longer read larger
than the contacts nested under them.
