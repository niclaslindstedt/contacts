---
type: Changed
title: Move the Archive button to the lower right of the side-menu action grid
---

The **Archive** button now sits in the lower-**right** corner of the side
menu's action grid instead of the lower left. The bottom row now reads
**Undo · Redo · Search · Archive**, tucking Archive into the corner where it
reads as a destination rather than a peer of the undo/redo controls. Its
drop-to-archive target and archived-count badge move with it.
