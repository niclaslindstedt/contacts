---
type: Changed
title: Clearer edit page — glyph section titles, shorter hints, tidier options
---

The contact **edit view** got a polish pass:

- **Section titles now carry a glyph** — a handset for phone numbers, an envelope
  for emails, a map pin for addresses, and so on — so each block scans at a
  glance, matching the marks the read view already wears on its rows.
- **The two set-once switches were renamed** to say what they do: **This is a
  company** is now **Company contact**, and the auto-archive switch **File this
  contact away automatically** is now **Time limited contact**.
- **Auto-archive moved to the very bottom** of the edit view, below the emergency
  and company switches — a set-and-forget schedule out of the way of the fields.
- **The option hints were shortened** so each fits two lines on a phone instead
  of running to four.
- **A company card now hides the private/work type toggle** on its phone numbers
  and emails — an organisation's numbers are all just the company's, with no
  private/work person behind them.
