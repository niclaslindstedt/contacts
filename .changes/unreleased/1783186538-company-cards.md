---
type: Changed
title: Company cards are simpler and export their name as the organisation
---

The **This is a company** switch now sits at the very bottom of the edit view,
beside the emergency flag, where it's set once and out of the way. Turning a card
into a company folds away the person-only fields — the **birthday** and the extra
**important dates** — since they don't apply to an organisation. A company also
always exports with its name as the vCard organisation (`ORG`), so it lands in
other address books as a company rather than a person.
