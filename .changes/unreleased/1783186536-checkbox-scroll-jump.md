---
type: Fixed
title: Toggling a switch at the bottom of a card no longer jumps the page
---

Flipping a switch near the bottom of a contact's edit view — **Emergency
contact**, **auto-archive**, or **This is a company** — no longer yanks the card
off-screen on desktop, leaving a big empty margin. The hidden checkbox behind
each switch is now anchored to the scrolling card body, so pressing it keeps the
card exactly where it is.
