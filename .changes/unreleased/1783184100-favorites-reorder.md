type: Added
title: Reorder your favorites by dragging
---

The **Favorites** page is now a hand-orderable shortlist. It shows your starred
contacts as one flat list — no longer split by folder — with a **grip handle**
on each row: drag a contact up or down to put the people you reach for most in
exactly the order you want. On a touch screen, press and hold the grip to pick a
row up, then drag.

The order is saved on each contact and syncs like any other edit, so it travels
with your address book. A newly starred contact joins the bottom of the list
until you drag it into place.
