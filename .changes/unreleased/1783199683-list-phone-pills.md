---
type: Changed
title: Phone numbers show as Private / Work pills in the List and Favorites
---

Phone numbers on the **List** and **Favorites** pages now read as **pills** — a
rounded chip that leads with the contact-method **glyph** (a person for
**Private**, a briefcase for **Work**) and then the number. Every number carries
its glyph now, not just rows with more than one, so a private number is easy to
tell from a work one at a glance. Tapping a pill still places the call. The rows
grow a little taller to give the pills room, which makes the two types easier to
scan.
