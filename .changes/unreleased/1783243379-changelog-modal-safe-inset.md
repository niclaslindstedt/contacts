type: Fixed
title: Keep the "What's new" and Learn more modal header clear of the notch
---

On a notched phone the **What's new** dialog — and the **Learn more**
feature-doc pages it opens — drew their header, and the close (×) button in it,
up under the status bar, so there was no way to tap it closed. The header now
carries the top safe-area inset, dropping its controls below the notch so the
dialog can always be dismissed.
