---
type: Changed
title: Density now shapes the settings, sidebar, and action island
---

The **Appearance → Density** knob reaches further than the contact list now.
Choosing a denser look tightens the whole app's chrome: the Settings cards —
the Appearance tab included — pull their sections and padding together, the side
menu's rows and its action-button island shrink, and the footer collapse rail
follows suit (a roomier setting spreads them all back out). So changing the
density visibly changes how the Appearance settings themselves look.

The Appearance tab also puts the **Colours** palette last, below the dialog
backdrop controls, so the long swatch grid no longer sits in the middle of the
tab.
