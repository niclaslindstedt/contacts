# Auto-archive

A contact can **file itself away on a date you pick** — handy for someone you
only need around for a while, like the pizzeria you add for a week's holiday and
want gone when you're home.

At the bottom of the edit view, the **Auto-archive** section carries a **Time
limited contact** switch. Flip it on, choose the date (it starts two weeks out so
nothing vanishes the moment you enable it), and choose what happens then:
**Archive** the card (shelve it, the default) or **Delete** it for good.

The schedule shows in read mode too ("Archives itself on 15 August 2026"), and
the sweep runs whenever you **open the app** — so a card whose date passed while
the app was closed catches up on your next visit, all as one undo step. Archiving
a card this way clears its schedule, so restoring it from the Archive won't
re-file it.
