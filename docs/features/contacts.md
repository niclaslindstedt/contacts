# Contact cards

Every contact is a card: a name, any number of phone numbers and email
addresses, a company, a free-form address, a birthday, notes, and a photo.

- **Edit in place.** Tap the name in the header to rename; every field commits
  when you leave it, and each committed edit is one undo step.
- **Photos.** Tap the avatar in the card header to upload a picture (downscaled
  on-device to a compact thumbnail) or pick an icon and accent colour instead.
- **Archive, don't lose.** Swipe a contact right in the side menu (or drag it
  onto Archive) to shelve it. The Archive page restores or deletes for good.
