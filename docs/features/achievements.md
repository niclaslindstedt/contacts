# Achievements

Every feature in the app is also a **trophy** to unlock. The trophy button at
the foot of the side menu opens the achievements tour — a guided list, grouped
into Beginner, Intermediate, Pro, and Expert tiers, of the things the app can
do. Each one you've earned lights up; each one you haven't hints at a corner you
haven't explored yet.

You unlock them just by using the app. Name your first contact, give someone
both a phone number and an email, star a favorite, flag an emergency contact,
nest a folder inside another, attach a file, connect a cloud drive, take a
backup, encrypt your synced copy, set a contact to archive itself on a date —
each is its own trophy, and there are more to find. When you earn one, a small
celebration names what you just did; the trophy button carries a badge until you
look.

The bigger trophies carry a **Learn more** line that explains the feature behind
them, so the tour doubles as a tour of the app. Prefer a quieter app? **Settings
→ General → Disable achievements** stops the tracking and hides the trophy
button — anything you've already earned is kept.

The whole tour follows your language, English or Swedish, along with the rest of
the interface.
